Brief History

Agra Fort, built by Emperor Akbar in 1565, served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors until 1638.

Why You Should Visit

Experience the architectural grandeur and rich history of one of India's most significant heritage sites, offering panoramic views and insights into Mughal architecture.
